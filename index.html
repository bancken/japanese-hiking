Hiking the Nakasendo trail

Join me on an epic hike following the old Nakasendo trail through the japanese heartlands

A brief history of the Nakasendo trail

The Nakasendo trail was used by samurai warriors and traders traveling between the old capital Edo and Kyoto. The road featured small posttowns where travellers could eat and rest over night.

How to get there

Fly to Tokyo, travel by bullet train or "shinkansen" from Tokyo to Nakatsugawa, then take the local bus to Magome post town.
